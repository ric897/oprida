1. Discover

    1 week

    We work with you to understand your vision, businesses and goals to plan the optimal solution. Here we'll plan a project scope and deliver a proposal.

  2. Design

    1 week

    We get to work designing the solution UI, UX, and architecture. We'll present wireframes, user flows, and styles before the development of the solution.

  3. Develop

    2-4 weeks.

    We'll utilize a sprint based development proccess to build your solution, checking in with you to ensure quality throughout the development period.

  4. Deploy

    1 week

    Hooray! It's time to launch! We'll meet with you to make sure we met all your requirements and deploy the project with a post-implementation support plan.
